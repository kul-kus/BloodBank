Benefits of Donating

Save Lives: One pint of blood can save up to 3 lives.

Health Screening: Free health checkup and blood test with each donation.

Refresh Your Body: Regular donation stimulates new blood cell production.

Reduce Health Risks: Regular donation can reduce risk of heart disease.

Emotional Satisfaction: Experience the joy of helping someone in need.

Free Refreshments: Complimentary snacks and beverages after donation.
